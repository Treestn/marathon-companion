Frequently Asked Questions

In the header of the app at the top of the screen, you can click on the PvP button, this will switch to PvE and a different progression will be loaded. To go back to the PvP progression, click on the same button.

In the header of the app at the top of the screen, you can click on the current bind, a pop-up window will open and you will be able to add any combination of CTRL, ALT, SHIFT + Letter that you desire. If the bind is incorrect, the box will be outlined in red. Otherwise, you can use the overwolf app and change it in the settings.

To cycle through the floors of a building, you can hover over the building, when the floor glows in white, it means you can click on it and the next floor will appear.

When a quest icon is on a different floor than the one displayed, you will see a green glow effect around the floor telling you a quest is on a different floor of the building.

If you want the app to stay on your desktop upon opening the game, you can go into settings and check the Desktop Only option.

You can join the discord with the discord icon at the top-right side of the screen and find the section bug-reports to do so.

To remove the pop-up, you can either check the don't show again button once the pop-up opens or go into the settings to enable or disable it.
