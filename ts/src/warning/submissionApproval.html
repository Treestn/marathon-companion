Submission Rules & Guidelines

Inappropriate Content: Any uploaded images must follow Overwolf's content policies. Submitting inappropriate images will result in a report to Overwolf.

Data Collection: The only information collected with your submission is your Overwolf username and Overwolf ID.

Anonymity: Your submission will be anonymous to other users.

Usage Rights: By submitting an image, you grant Marathon Companion the right to use the image within the app.

Submission Limits: Maximum 2 submissions per 5 minutes.

Image Submission Limits: Maximum 10 images per 5 minutes.

Image Requirements: Must have a 16:9 aspect ratio and must not exceed 8 MB in file size

Violations & Bans: Breaking these rules will result in a ban from submitting content.

By continuing, you confirm that you have read and agreed to these conditions.
